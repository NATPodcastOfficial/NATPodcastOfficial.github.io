In the information below, you will find a Purpose section that explains why we need such rules, then below that is the Preface section. If you have any questions about our server rules, please email us or text one of our server administrators/moderators for assistance.

Purpose

In Not Atomically Thinking, it is our mission to keep everyone in the server safe and welcoming for everyone. On a few occasions, people might join just to threaten server members using hate speech, racisim (racist slurs, etc.) and then just leave the server as if that nothing will happen to them. But thanks to our highly trained moderators, administrators, head of staff and supervisors, they have trained for this moment and for anyone who does such an act, their punishment will be given to them accordingly, so that you may have a valuable experience at our server. In the Rules section, it will outline most of the "what to do and what not to do", in the Discord Server. We incorage that everyone reads the rules so that they can protect themselves and so they can know what is right, and what is wrong.

Preface

⚠ Remember: All rules in the server apply at all times and in all places. Most of the time, broken rules result in a warning - these expire with time and add up to punishments such as a mute or ban. Certain rules that are broken may come with an immediate ban or a permanent ban.

Propagating or influencing those that have broken the rules may result in punishment. This includes attempting to bait people into admitting they're under 13, trying to make people spam ping you through a nickname, or quoting/replying to rule-breaking messages. Basically, trying people to admit the wrong when they are innocent.

For moderation purposes, this server is English only. This means you cannot use ASCII, binary, morse code, ciphered code, or made-up languages from games/movies.

⚠ WARNING: If anybody copies any part of rules expect those that are mentioned in Discord ToS and those who copy any trademark of Not Atomically Thinking, will be permanently banned from the server with no way of getting back in. This type of ban is unappealable.

We must also uphold Discord's Terms of Service and Community Guidelines

Server Rules

• Treat everyone with respect. Absolutely no harassment, witch hunting, sexism, racism, or hate speech will not be tolerated.

• No spam or self-promotion (server invites, advertisements, etc) without permission from a staff member. This includes DMing fellow members.

• No NSFW or obscene content. This includes text, images, or links featuring nudity, sex, hard violence, or other graphically disturbing content.

• Begging to gain a role is not accepted. If you consecutively ask for the role, you might be kicked because you are wasting time of my staff and you are not being respectful.

• If you see something against the rules or something that makes you feel unsafe, let staff know. Even if somebody calls you a snitch, it is their punishment and we can even reward you for making this server a safe place. We want this server to be a welcoming space! • Please keep your Discord Username in English or have it contain with no characters that are not on the US Keyboard

• If there is anybody disrespecting staff there shall be a PERMANENT BAN against the user.
